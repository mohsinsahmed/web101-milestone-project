The Equity Gala – Event Schedule

5:30 PM – Guest Arrival & Check-In
Welcome drinks, valet service, and elegant photo ops upon entry.

6:00 PM – Networking & Hors d’Oeuvres
Mingle with fellow investors, industry professionals, and sponsors over curated appetizers.

6:45 PM – Opening Remarks
A short welcome … “Building Wealth, One Property at a Time.”

7:00 PM – Keynote Speaker: The Power of Equity
An inspiring talk from a renowned real estate investor or wealth strategist.

7:45 PM – Dinner Service
Three-course plated meal served with soft music and ambient lighting.

8:30 PM – Panel Discussion: Real Estate in 2025 and Beyond
Live Q&A with experts in commercial, residential, and multifamily investments.

9:15 PM – Awards & Recognition
Honoring standout contributors and rising stars in the real estate community.

9:45 PM – Closing Remarks & Call to Action
Final thoughts and next steps.

10:00 PM – After-Hours Social
Optional mixer for continued networking.
